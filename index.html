Munder Difflin is a publicly owned paper company. We have been helping companies fulfill their paper needs since 1908. In 1963 we expanded into office supplies in general, and have been the main supplier for major corporations. We have been in business for over 100 years and are looking to forward to continuing this company's great tradition.
